Ammonite LLC Intermodal Logistics

Ammonite LLC is a shipping company offering intermodal logistics services to traders, retailers, freight forwarders, shipping lines and related groups. The project was commissioned to give their team a consolidated, real-time view of inventory and trading data replacing fragmented manual reporting with a live analytics dashboard.

Ammonite LLC operates in container logistics and therefore requires realtime information across multiple data streams like commodity markets, freight pricing indices, geopolitical calendars but had no single platform to bring these together. Decision-making around inventory levels, trading prices and logistics planning required pulling information from disparate sources manually.

I designed and built a comprehensive Python dashboard that integrates all of these data sources into a unified interface. Each tab targets a specific operational need from high-level inventory snapshots and sales/cost breakdowns to live commodity prices, port activity, global event calendars and news pulled directly from the company Telegram channel.

Project Goals
📡
Real-Time Decision Support
Provide Ammonite LLC with live insights across inventory, trading prices, commodity markets and global events enabling informed, timely decisions without manual data collection.
⚡
Operational Efficiency
Streamline the process of tracking inventory movement, analysing sales and costs and monitoring port activity reducing manual reporting effort and accelerating analysis cycles.
Outcome

The platform delivered a consolidated, real-time analytics hub for Ammonite LLC replacing fragmented manual data collection with a live dashboard that brings together internal inventory data, commodity markets, freight pricing, global events and company communications in one place.

By making data from five distinct sources instantly accessible and visually presented, the app reduced the time Ammonite's team spends gathering and reconciling information allowing them to focus on what the data means for inventory decisions and trading strategy rather than on collecting it.
